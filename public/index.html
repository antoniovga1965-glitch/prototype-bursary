Why Choose Smart Bursary?

Traditional bursary systems are slow, opaque, and vulnerable to corruption. Students miss out on deserved scholarships while duplicates and forged applications slip through.

Smart Bursary changes that. Using AI-powered document verification, duplicate detection, and income cross-referencing, we ensure every shilling reaches the right student.

No connections needed. No corruption. Just fair access.

How It Works

1

Apply

Submit your application with required documents (KCSE cert, ID, birth cert, proof of income)

2

Verify

Our AI scans and verifies your documents in real-time. No manual delays, no room for manipulation

3

Detect

We check for duplicates and inconsistencies across applications. One student, one scholarship

4

Approve

County officials review verified applications with full transparency. You'll know exactly why you were approved or flagged

5

Receive

Approved funds go directly to you via M-Pesa. No middlemen, no delays
